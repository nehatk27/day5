About:

Company History:

2004:
Founded in Kochi, Kerala. Luminar TechnoLab launched its first batch of 15 students focused on basic Python and Web Development.
2010:
Expanded curriculum to include full-stack web development and established a dedicated placement cell, successfully placing 90% of graduates.
2015:
Partnered with major IT firms in Kochi and Bengaluru, allowing students access to real-world corporate software projects during training..
2021:
Launched an AI and Machine Learning boot camp, fully migrating the learning environment to cloud-based, hands-on coding modules.
2026:
Celebrating 22 years of tech education with over 9,000+ alumni employed at top global tech companies worldwide.

Values:

Commitment to Continuous Innovation

In a rapidly evolving tech landscape, we believe that education is a lifelong pursuit rather than a one-time event. We are dedicated to fostering a growth-oriented mindset, empowering both our learners and trainers to constantly adapt, unlearn, and master emerging technologies. By staying ahead of industry curves, we ensure our curriculum remains at the forefront of modern software development.

Industry-Driven Practical Excellence

We bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world execution. Our training programs prioritize hands-on experience, agile problem-solving, and clean coding practices to prepare professionals for the demands of the global IT Sector. We measure our success not just by concepts taught, but by the tangible career milestones and project deliverables our students achieve.

Inclusive Growth and Collaboration

Technology thrives on diverse perspectives and collective intelligence. We are dedicated to cultivating a psychologically safe, inclusive, and collaborative training environment where everyone feels valued and respected. By breaking down complex concepts and encouraging open dialogue, we empower individuals from all backgrounds to unlock their full potential and shape the future of tech.
